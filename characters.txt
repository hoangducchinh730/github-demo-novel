Main Characters:

- Clara Bennett: 17 years old, curious, introverted, with a knack for solving puzzles.
- Elias Thorn: Mysterious traveler, possibly from another realm, protector of ancient knowledge.
- Mrs. Whitmore: Bookstore owner, wise and cryptic, knows more than she lets on.
- The Watchers: Shadowy figures that appear when forbidden knowledge is uncovered.
